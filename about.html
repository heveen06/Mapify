About Us

Welcome to Mapify

We are TriCode, a team of three passionate web developers
Heveen Almohammad, Helen Ali, and Lana Ali. Our project Mapify is an interactive geography website designed to make learning about the world more engaging, fun, and easy.

About Our Project

Mapify allows users to explore a clickable world map where each country reveals information about its history, culture, geography, and key facts. We also include historical maps, trivia quizzes, rankings, and user accounts to make geography more interactive and competitive. Our goal is to make learning enjoyable for students, teachers, and anyone curious about the world.
